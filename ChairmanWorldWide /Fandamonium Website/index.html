FAQ

Is this a Discord clone?

No. Discord is chat-first. Fandamonium is voting + threads first, with cleaner structure and discovery.

How do you stop spam and brigading?

One-vote-per-user, rate limits, anomaly detection on vote velocity, and moderation tools.

Will you use Spotify/Apple Music data?

Yes — artwork and metadata via APIs. Playback depends on licensing and platform rules.
